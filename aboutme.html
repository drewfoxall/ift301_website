Welcome to my webpage! I am currently a college student at Arizona State University, studying Information Technology. Outside of that, have a strong passion for gaming, cars and photography. This is the webpage that I've created to display my photography. I specifically love taking photos of cars, nature, and quite frankly anything that I find beautiful in nature. I hope that you enjoy the photos I have taken!

I love to walk through nature and take it all in one step at a time. My journey of photography has done a lot in the ways of teaching me to slow down, and appreciate what is around us. This is my main goal when it comes to photography, to spread some of the beauty that is around us every day, but that we may sometimes turn an accidental blind eye to.
